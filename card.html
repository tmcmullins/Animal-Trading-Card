Bengal Tiger

Tigers roars can reach 114dB due to their large, flat vocal folds.

  • Weight: 310 - 490lbs
  • Length: 8 - 10ft
  • Top Speed: 60mph
  • Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Habitat: India

Tigers are orange with white belly areas. They are covered in non-uniformed black stripes which camaflouge them in the jungle environment. Tigers are the largest cats in the world with weights ranging over 700lbs.
